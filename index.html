Backstory

Growing up, I listened to many different types of music. With My dad it was 1960's,70's and 80's classic rock , and with my mom it was 70's and 80's disco, pop and soul music. When I was younger It was sort of against my will because what would just be what was playing. But as I got older I started to adopt my parents music taste and would make playlists full of the songs they played. more recently I discovered my own music taste. which still included those old songs, but with the new addition of R&B music. I develped such a love for R&B music, specifically from the 90's.

What does it mean to me?/importance

The reason my parents music is important to me is because it reminds me of being a child. It also reflects how different they are as people and how differently they grew up. R&B music is important to me because it is mainly the music I like to sing. All the songs have a certain similar beat and chords that I think is so addicting. It also represents me having a music taste of my own.
